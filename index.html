Women in Film

Putting a spotlight on the female voice in film.

Welcome to Women in Film!

Women in Film is dedicated to raising awareness about the female experience in the male-dominated film industry. This site focuses on many aspects of this from delineating the history of women in film to showcasing prominent actresses in the field today to exhibiting movies with strong female roles both past and present. Here you can learn something new through a variety of mediums.
